About Us
The idea was very simple,decided and dedicated to provide Maritime Education and Training to the Indian youths aspiring their career in Merchant Navy. About 02 decade prior, people from the northen region has to travel to west or Southern part of India for Marine Education and Training. This institute was launched with a sole objective to spread/transfer and create Maritime Education among our society and to provide man power to Indian and International maritime.
